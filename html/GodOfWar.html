The Saga

From Ghost of Sparta to Father of the North

Once a Spartan commander bound to Ares, Kratos rose from betrayal and grief to challenge gods themselves. His journey winds from Olympus to the realms of Norse myth, where restraint and redemption reshape a brutal past. Side by side with his son, Atreus, he learns to temper rage with wisdom.
